How to get there

The wedding location is Fort bij Vechten, a beautiful fort close to Utrecht. The fort is part of the UNESCO World Heritage site "Hollandse Waterlinie".

By car

You can navigate to Achterdijk 12, 3981 HB Bunnik. There is ample parking in front of the fort (â‚¬4,- per day). If there's space you can also park for free near Restaurant Vroeg, a few minutes walking from the fort, here.

By public transport

The fort is easily accesible from Utrecht Centraal. You can either rent an OV-fiets and an 18 minutes cycle trip will get you there. Alternatively, you can take Bus 41 to Wijk bij Duurstede. You need to step off at Oud-Amelisweerd and it is a 5 minutes walk from there.

Where to stay

Here are some nearby options to stay overnight:

  • For a great camping option: Buitengoed De Boomgaard (20min walk from location)
  • For an option right next to Utrecht Central station: Crowne Plaza (18min cycle)
  • For a simple but hip hotel right next to the city centre: Moxy Utrecht (15min cycle)
  • For options with free parking: Van der Valk - De Bilt (12min drive), Postillion Hotel Utrecht Bunnik (6min drive)

Many other great options can be found on AirBnB or via booking.com!


Additional information

Is there a dresscode?

Not really, you can wear whatever you consider wedding appropriate. If the weather allows, the ceremony will be held outside on a grass field. So if the soil is a bit wet, stiletto heels might not be the best choice.

Do you have any wishes for gifts?

To us, your presence is more than enough! If you would nonetheless like to give us a gift, we would appreciate a financial contribution to our increasingly bourgeois lifestyle: a bakfiets to be able to cycle around with Mads.

Can I contribute to the wedding program?

We don't want a big program, so that there is enough free time for people to mingle, chat, and dance. However, if you would like to give a speech, sing a song or have something else in mind, please get in touch with our masters of ceremony Jesse Voetman and Annique Claringbould via ejenlara@gmail.com.
